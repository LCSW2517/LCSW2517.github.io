Meet the CEO

John Matthews is the visionary founder and CEO of ArticVault. With over 15 years of experience in outdoor equipment innovation, he has a deep passion for creating solutions that enhance outdoor adventures. John’s journey began as an avid camper and outdoor enthusiast, where he saw the need for a more reliable and efficient cooler to withstand extreme conditions while keeping food and beverages fresh. His dedication to quality and performance drives the mission to provide the best cooling solutions for every adventurer.

Under his leadership, ArticVault has grown from a passionate startup into a recognized leader in outdoor cooling solutions, known for its innovation and premium quality products. John Matthews fosters a collaborative company culture where each team member’s input is highly valued. His dedication to sustainability and cutting-edge design powers ArcticVault’s mission to deliver eco-friendly, high-performance coolers that enhance outdoor experiences and keep your essentials fresh, no matter where the adventure takes you.
